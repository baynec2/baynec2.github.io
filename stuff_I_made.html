3D Printing

I have recently been getting interested in 3D printing. I have an Anycubic Vyper 3d printer, and I have been very impressed with the technology.

I like to use 3D printing to make devices that make my life easier in the lab. So far, I have mostly made stands to support various workflows, I intend to continue to design and publish useful things as they come up. You can find my designs published here.

I have recently decided to open an Etsy store to sell some of stuff that I can make using this technology. Right now, I am planning on selling 3D printed wall decor of custom ordered geolocations. I have always thought wall decor like this is super cool, but pretty unapproachable in terms of cost. I think that I can bring these high quality art fixtures to people for a fraction of the price of alternatives. Check out what is avalible at my store, CoordinateDesigns for yourself here: here.

Web Apps

I have created a couple pretty simple web apps. I usually use R shiny to create them. Most of them aren't useful enough to publish, but here are ones that I thought were cool.

Wordflock is an app that lets you make a word cloud from tweets of a given user. Sometimes it is interesting to see what words are most often on the mind of a notable public figure, or even just a friend. You can find it here

TRPV1_GO_Coexpression_Explorer is an app that I made to help out my friend Felix with his thesis work. He was interested in mining publically avalible data for his own purposes. I built an app that functions to facillitate this, take a look at it here
